Bank Loans for Startups

Explore various bank loan options available for startups.

Loan Option 1: Small Business Administration (SBA) Loan

Amount: Up to $5 million

Interest Rate: Competitive rates based on market.

Eligibility: Must meet certain credit and business criteria.

Loan Option 2: Equipment Financing

Amount: Up to 100% of equipment costs

Interest Rate: Varies based on lender.

Eligibility: Must provide collateral (equipment).

Loan Option 3: Business Line of Credit

Amount: Up to $1 million

Interest Rate: Variable rates apply.

Eligibility: Good credit score required.

Loan Option 4: Short-term Business Loan

Amount: Up to $250,000

Interest Rate: Higher rates for shorter terms.

Eligibility: Fast approval for urgent needs.

Loan Option 5: Commercial Real Estate Loan

Amount: Varies by property value

Interest Rate: Fixed or variable rates available.

Eligibility: Property must be for business use.

Loan Option 6: Franchise Financing

Amount: Varies based on franchise requirements

Interest Rate: Competitive rates for franchises.

Eligibility: Must be approved by franchisor.

Loan Option 7: Agricultural Loans

Amount: Up to $1 million

Interest Rate: Varies based on lender and type.

Eligibility: Must be for agricultural purposes.

Loan Option 8: Working Capital Loans

Amount: Up to $500,000

Interest Rate: Rates based on creditworthiness.

Eligibility: Fast access to cash for expenses.

Loan Option 9: Start-up Business Loans

Amount: Up to $150,000

Interest Rate: Rates vary based on lender.

Eligibility: Business plan required.

Loan Option 10: Merchant Cash Advances

Amount: Varies based on sales

Interest Rate: Higher rates for fast access.

Eligibility: Requires daily credit card sales.
